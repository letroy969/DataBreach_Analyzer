Dataset Information

This dataset contains 500+ synthetic data breach records with 14 columns of information, designed for Power BI dashboard creation and advanced analytics.

2020-2024 (5 years of data)
Industries
Financial, Healthcare, Education, Technology, Transportation, Retail, Energy, Media, Government, Manufacturing
Countries
20+ countries across North America, Europe, Asia, Oceania, and South America
Breach Types
Insider, Hacking, System Error, Physical, Social Engineering, Unknown
Severity Levels
Catastrophic, Critical, High, Medium, Low
